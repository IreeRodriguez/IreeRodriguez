Meet
Irene Rodríguez

Hi, my name is Irene and if I had to describe myself in a single word I would, without a doubt, choose "pragmatism". I am a direct and honest person. I believe that hard work, constant preparation and willingness to learn are the bases that make up my philosophy of life and my professional ethics. Therefore, when I get involved in a project I am methodical, entrepreneurial and persistent. I give priority to drawing up an action plan with short-term goals, always with a view to achieving the final goal; I think it is the best course of action to maximize efficiency and work performance. I am fascinated by the feeling of giving the best of myself, achieving my goal and continuing to learn as much as I can.
